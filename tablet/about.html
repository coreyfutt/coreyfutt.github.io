About Me

Hi, my name is Corey Futterman. I am a graduate of the class of 2015 at Binghamton University with a Bachelor of Arts in Graphic Design.

Designing is what I do, and I love what I do. I strive for excellence in everything I do and expect only that. I have a natural eye for design and a conscientious attention to detail, some would call me a perfectionist. However, I would describe myself as an efficient, detail-oriented, and creative designer.

I currently work as the Art Director at ResearchConnection. I've worked as a graphic designer for the Binghamton University library and the BU Pipe Dream. I have previously worked for G3 Communications.

You can find out more about my work experience on my resume page or my PDF resume. And if you feel like finding more about myself and my graphic design career, check out my contact page and send me an email.
